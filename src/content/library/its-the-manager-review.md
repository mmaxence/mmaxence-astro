## Leadership Lessons

### From Boss to Coach

In the high-velocity Seoul market, my role is to coach designers to be resilient during pivots.

## How It Shaped My Work

This book reinforced my belief that management is about coaching, not directing. In the fast-paced, pivot-heavy environment of a Korean startup, I've learned to coach designers through uncertainty rather than trying to control outcomes. This approach has built a more resilient, adaptable team.
